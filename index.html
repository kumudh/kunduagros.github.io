Kundu Agros Corn Silage

Kundu Agros considers corn crop as best for silage making. Corn plant has  broad leaves and thick stems, so we consider this as suitable for Silage making. 
Silage is feremented, high-moistured stored fodder which can be fed to cattle, sheep and other cud-chewing animals.
By adopting new methodologies and machinary, Kundhu Agros develops high quality Silage using advanced technology.

WHY CORN SILAGE ?

1

Increase in milk production

Milk yielding gradually increases with the usage of silage.

2

Animal Digestion Improves

By using silage regularly, animal digestion improves.

3

Low Price with Good Nutrients

Silage is low cost and contains abundant nutrients.

4

Durability

Silage bag can be witheld upto 18 months.

5

Reduce Cost and Labour

Silage bag occupies less space when compared to other feeds.

6

Idle for all climatic conditions

Silage can be given to animals all around the year.
